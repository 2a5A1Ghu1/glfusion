Story Submissions

This is the command and control quick moderation area for story submissions.  In this area you can edit, approve or delete story submissions.  To edit a story, click on the Edit icon. To approve or delete story submissions, select one  or more checkboxes and click on the appropriate action icon.

Field  Description
Edit Click to view or edit the story
Title  Title of the story
Date Date the story was submitted
Topic Topic the story was submitted to
