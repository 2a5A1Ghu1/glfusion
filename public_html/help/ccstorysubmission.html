Story Submissions

This is the command and control quick moderation area for story submissions.  In this area you can edit (approve) and delete story submissions.  Select any deletes and click the submit button.
Field  Description
edit Click this to edit the story, you must edit to approve a story
Title  Title of the story
Date Date the story was submitted
Topic Topic the story was submitted to
Delete Radio button to select for deleting a story submission
